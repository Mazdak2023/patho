How to Use This Quiz

1

Choose a Case Study

Select from various pathophysiology topics organized by clinical scenarios. Each case contains multiple questions related to the topic.

2

Answer Questions

Read each scenario carefully and select your answer(s). Some questions have multiple correct answers - look for the note at the top of the question.

3

Submit & Learn

For single-answer questions, click your choice to see instant feedback. For multiple-choice questions, select all that apply and click "Submit Answer". You'll receive detailed explanations to help you understand the concept better.

4

Track Progress

Monitor your performance with the progress bar and statistics. Return to the main menu anytime to see your overall score and choose new topics.
